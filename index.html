Our Journey

What started as a simple idea in 2002—using sport to keep girls safe and give them hope—has grown into a thriving community organization. From a single soccer team to hundreds of students supported through education, life skills, and sports, KGSA continues to transform lives.

What We Do

⚽ Games & Sports

Soccer, tennis, rugby, and netball programs designed to build confidence, teamwork, and physical health.

📚 Higher Education

High school support, college and university preparation to help girls reach their academic goals.

🌟 Student Development

Life skills development, mentorship, and personal growth programs for holistic empowerment.
