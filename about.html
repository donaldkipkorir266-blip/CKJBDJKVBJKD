About Kibera Girls Soccer Academy

Our Story, Mission, and Commitment to Excellence

Our Belief System

Local Solutions

We believe that local problems require local solutions. We recognize the expertise of community leaders and invest in their ability to create meaningful change from within.

The Girl Effect

We believe in the power of the girl effect: investing in girls transforms their lives and uplifts families and communities. When girls are empowered, entire generations benefit.

Child Protection

Child protection is a core part of our work. We ensure that every girl receives a quality education in a safe, respectful, and supportive environment.

Sustainable Innovation

When local leaders are empowered, they develop innovative and sustainable solutions to challenges such as poverty. We empower, don't rescue.

Our History

2002

The Beginning

A community coach starts a girls' soccer team in Kibera with a simple vision: use sport to keep girls safe and give them hope.

2006

Expansion to Education

The project expands into a one-room school with thirteen students. We begin integrating education with sports development.

2015

Growth & Recognition

KGSA grows to support hundreds of students through education, life skills, and sports. We become recognized as a key organization in Kibera.

2026

Today

Over 500 girls have benefited from KGSA programs. We continue to expand our reach and impact in the community.
